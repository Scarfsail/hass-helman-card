# The idea

The idea is to show a node detail modal window when I click on a node on the helman-simple-card component.
The detail will show appropriate information for the node.

# The node types and details

## Battery

- Current power
- Curent SoC
- Current mode [idle, charging, discharging]
- Time to full charge / discharge
- When the battery will be charged / discharged
- Min and max SoC
- Remaining capacity in kWh
- Today's kWh charged / discharged

## House

* Current power
* Today's consumed energy in kWh

## Solar Panel

- Current power
- Today's produced energy in kWh
- Remaining forecasted energy in kWh

## Grid

* Current power
* Today's imported energy in kWh
* Today's exported energy in kWh
